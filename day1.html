Making Tea

  1. Boil water in a pot.
  1. Add tea leaves and sugar.
  1. Let it simmer for a few minutes.

  2. Strain the tea into a cup and add milk if desired.

Making Paratha and Aloo Curry

  1. Knead the dough for paratha and Let it rest.
  1. Roll out the dough and cook on a hot griddle with oil or ghee.
  1. For aloo curry, saute onions,tomatoes,and spices in a pan.

  2. Add boiled potatoes and cook until the curry thickens.

Making Biryani

  1. Marinate the meat or vegetables with spices and yogurt.
  1. Cook basmati rice until its 70% done.
  1. Layer the Marinated meat/vegetables and rice in a pot.

  2. Cook on low heat(dum) until fully cooked.
